# Overview

Your **inbox** contains messages, notifications and actions specific to you.

# Messages

[Messages](/guide/inbox/messages) are private one-on-one or group communications with other users of HASH.

# Notifications

[Notifications](/guide/inbox/notifications) make you aware of other key information. Some notifications require action, while others may be purely informational messages which you can either read or ignore.

# Actions

[Actions](/guide/inbox/actions) consist of things that are awaiting your attention. AI suggestions, proposed edits and updates from other users, requests to review things, your own unfinished drafts, pending todos, and more.
