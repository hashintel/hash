# HashGPT

**HashGPT** lets you access information from your [web(s)](/guide/webs) via the official `chat.openai.com` ChatGPT interface. To use it, you'll need to install the _HashGPT_ app from the GPT Store, and connect your HASH account.

**AI Chat** in HASH lets you interact with an AI assistant who can query your web, as well as create and manage [workers](/guide/ai/workers) and [flows](/guide/flows). Interactive, rich, detailed responses are rendered using [blocks](/guide/blocks).
