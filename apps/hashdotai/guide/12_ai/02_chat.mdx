# HashGPT<a id="#hashgpt" /><a id="#chatgpt" />

**HashGPT** lets you access information from your [web(s)](/guide/webs) via the official `chat.openai.com` ChatGPT interface. To use it, you'll need to open [HashGPT](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-i9t0QlD9c-hashgpt) in the GPT Store, and connect your HASH account.

# AI Chat in HASH

<Callout style="information" type="coming-soon">
  AI Chat _inside_ HASH is coming soon. If you'd like to help beta test this
  feature, [create an account](/signup) and we'll let you know when it's ready.

**AI Chat** in HASH lets you interact with an AI assistant who can query your web, as well as create and manage [workers](/guide/ai/workers) and [flows](/guide/flows). Interactive, rich, detailed responses are rendered using [blocks](/guide/pages/blocks).
